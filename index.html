Seven Riverdale Facts

Are you an Archie comics fan ?
Let's just say that this dark story drama series will have you on the edge of your seat in so many more ways than the corresponding comic books would ever do. The story is about a town, her name is Riverdale. The town is divided into two rival parts, the rich north side and the poor south side. Following the tragic death of a beloved classmate during the summer, teenager Archie and his friends Betty, Veronica and Jughead tried to solve the murder mistery.
